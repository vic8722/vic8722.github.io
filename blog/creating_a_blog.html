Biography

The process of creating a blog has definitely been quite the learning experience for me. It reminds me of the late 90s when making a 'Xanga' page was the most popular thing to do. Like back then, this process is equally as frustrating for me now.

I find myself underwhelmed with the whole process. As a more 'left-brained' type person, I am not very into asthetics. I find spending time trying to make the websites look good and display nicely to be somewhat tedious.

Also, for whatever reason, the whole process does not come nearly as intuitively to me as say Ruby or JavaScript. I spent a good few hours today trying to figure out how to use Bootstrap and Jekyll, but ultimately got annoyed with the entire process and gave up.

I'm sure later on I'll come back to it and spend some more time making this website look nice. But until then, I will focus my energy on the other aspects of web development that I've been enjoying more.
