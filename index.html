Victoria Solorzano

You can call me Vic

I'm a writer and stand-up comedian based in Brooklyn.

Work

I love working with people in the arts. I work with a lot of drag artists and other performers in NYC and LA doing video editing, costume creation, event coordination, tour planning, and life admin. Recent collaborators include IMHO, Darby Lynn Cartwright, The Bad Judies, Skye Walker, and GOONIVERSITY Podcast.

I wrote, directed, and produced my own parody musical Wait Sis' with the New York Theater Festival.

Now

I recently discovered Stamptown a month ago and fell in love with the whole concept. I have watched the YouTube special so many times I think I could run the buttons. I haven't watched much clown but I see a lot of stand up and it's the most fun, magical experience I've had in years. It keeps me up at night trying to put together how it all works with the timing of what's planned vs improvised.

I've always focused on the writing part of my stand-up and never wanted much of an on-stage persona, but now all I want to do is figure out how to bring the kind of energy Jack does to the stage.

I am taking time away from tech work (fuck AI) and ultimately hope to end up working with and supporting creatives. I can think of no better use of that time than observing how everything comes together night after night in Edinburgh. I'm a very capable, adaptable human and would be happy to assist in any capacity for the opportunity to absorb what I can.
