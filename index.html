Theater, Comedy & Fringe

I'm a comedian and theater producer from Brooklyn, coming to Edinburgh for my first Fringe Festival in August. I've been involved in theater and performance my whole life — tap dancing since I was 4, stand up since 2018, and for the past three years I've been working in NYC and LA's drag and nightlife scene, most notably with IMHO the Show. I also recently produced my own drag musical Wait Sis' for the NY Theater Festival. I have several stand up friends performing this year, and I'm excited to catch their shows, discover new acts, and experience the full magic of Fringe. I'll be out exploring and catching shows most of the time.
